Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

Mediterranean Chickpea Salad is a vibrant and refreshing salad bursting with Mediterranean flavors. It's packed with protein-rich chickpeas, crunchy vegetables, and a zesty lemon-herb dressing. Perfect as a light lunch or a side dish for any meal.

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your nutrition plans different from others?

Our nutrition plans are personalized based on your individual needs, dietary preferences, and lifestyle. We focus on sustainable habits rather than short-term solutions.

Are your meal plans suitable for vegetarians or vegans?

Yes! We offer customized meal plans that cater to vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary preferences.

How long does it take to see results with your nutrition plan?

Results vary, but most clients start seeing improvements within a few weeks, especially when following the plan consistently along with an active lifestyle.

Do I need to count calories while following your plan?

No, our focus is on nutrient-dense foods and mindful eating rather than strict calorie counting. We guide you on portion control in a way that feels natural.

Is there support if I have questions after starting the plan?

Absolutely! We offer continuous support through email or chat to ensure you stay on track and answer any questions you may have.
